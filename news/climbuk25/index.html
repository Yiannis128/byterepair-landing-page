Climb UK 25

We are going to visit and present at Climb UK.

Yiannis Charalambous is set to present Byte Repair at Climb UK 25, aiming to introduce the company as a promising start-up to the UK investment community and a wide range of businesses. This presentation will highlight Byte Repair’s innovative approach and growth potential, positioning it as an emerging player in the technology sector.

Climb UK 25 serves as a leading platform for start-ups, investors, and business leaders to connect, share insights, and explore new opportunities. For more information about the event and its broader mission, visit the Climb UK website.
